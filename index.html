Kriya Neuro

Technologies Pvt Ltd

Dedicated to helping people lead fuller, healthier lives, Kriyaneuro Technologies is an R&D company that specialises in studying human brain activity and its application in developing devices and therapies for people with neural and motor disorders.

We work to improve diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation for patients with various neuro-motor disabilities. Further, we also offer solutions to companies interested in improving their marketing strategies by understanding how their advertisements can affect the neural activity of potential customers.

About Us

Kriyaneuro Technologies is a biomedical research company that develops technical aids for patients suffering from motor neuron disorders, chiefly parkinson's disease, or stroke.

Know more..

What we do?

Kriyaneuro Technologies is a private company committed to developing assistive technologies for people with neural and motor disorders. We produce devices that facilitate more efficient diagnosis and help in providing customized treatment for patients dealing with diseases like parkinsons. Our vision is to make better treatment at affordable prices available to the patients who are in need.
